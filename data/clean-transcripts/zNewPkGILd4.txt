# 20110304-zNewPkGILd4-How to Sync Your Calendar to Tungle.me

Kind: captions
Language: en
Connecting your calendar to TungleMe
allows you to see your calendar events
in your account and for Tungle to keep
your public profile up to date and
automatically prevent double bookings
for you.
It's important to note that none of your
calendar details are shared publicly on
your public profile. Only your free busy
information is shared.
To connect a calendar to TungleMe, go to
the calendar dropdown.
and select sync calendar contacts.
Click add new calendar and contact sync
source
and select the calendar type you'd like
to connect.
Follow the simple steps and you're done.
