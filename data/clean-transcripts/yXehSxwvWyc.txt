# 20101111-yXehSxwvWyc-How to set your default calendar in Tungle.me

Kind: captions
Language: en
You can connect multiple calendars to
Tungle. However, meetings booked in
Tungle will only be added to one of
those calendars. This is your default
calendar. Here's how to set it.
Click calendars and contacts on the
left. You'll see all the calendars
you've connected.
Select the calendar that you want to
make default
from the drop down.
Click save changes.
And you're done.
